Critical Cyber Threats We Defend Against

Phishing Attacks

Social engineering attempts to steal user data or credentials. Our defense: Security Training & Email Gateways.

Ransomware

Malicious software encrypts data, demanding payment. Our defense: Robust Backup & Incident Response.

Supply Chain Attacks

Exploiting vulnerabilities in third-party vendors for network access. Our defense: Vendor Risk Management & Managed SOC.

Zero-Day Exploits

Attacks exploiting unknown software vulnerabilities before a patch is released. Our defense: Advanced Threat Intelligence & EDR.

Insider Threats

Security risks originating from negligent or malicious current/former employees. Our defense: Access Control & Security Audits.

DDoS Attacks

Overwhelming systems with traffic to disrupt service availability. Our defense: Cloud-based Mitigation & Network Hardening.
